This blog strategy is designed to drive high-intent organic traffic from recruiters, startup founders, and technical managers searching for Flutter and React development expertise.

## Core Pillars
1. **Flutter Mobile Development** (40%)
2. **React & Web Development** (30%)
3. **Full-Stack & AWS Architecture** (20%)
4. **Career & Freelance Journey** (10%)

## Execution Plan
1. **Frequency:** Publish 1 article every two weeks on Dev.to.
2. **Syndication:** Cross-post on Medium and LinkedIn with canonical tags pointing back to the Dev.to article.
3. **Portfolio Integration:** The dynamic blog section on the portfolio will automatically fetch and display the latest Dev.to posts, driving organic SEO value to the portfolio while maintaining content centrally.
